About Me

Researcher, Engineer, Problem Solver

I'm a Computer Science Engineering graduate from Jaypee University of Engineering and Technology, driven by a deep curiosity for scientific computing and data-driven research. My journey has taken me through research internships at ISRO's National Remote Sensing Centre, where I worked on lightning forecasting systems using satellite data, and at DRDO's Defence Research Development Establishment, where I built deep-learning models for air quality prediction achieving up to 96% accuracy.

With 3 published research papers in numerical modelling and Monte Carlo simulation, I'm passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical computation and real-world applications. I'm now joining BITS Pilani, Goa Campus as a Pre-Doc Researcher, where I look forward to pushing the boundaries of remote sensing, deep learning, and scientific computing.
